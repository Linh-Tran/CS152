<h2> <u>My Favorite Places</u> </h2>
<p style="margin-right:20px"> In 2004 my mom, sister, and I went to Saigon,Vietnam to visit our family. That was the first time I ever been there. The lively streets and the fact that there was street food in every street corner fascinated me. The most memorable moment was at Dam Sen Water Park. I remembered the thrill of riding the slides. In addition, the city was spectacular at night. Lights emitted from markets, streets, and resturants. Surprisingly, stores and markets were still open at 10 o'clock at night. Overall, Vietnam was a 
nice experience and I wish to visit there again.  
</p>
